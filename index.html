Objectives
🪙Collect coins and open chests for score & artifacts
⚔Defeat enemies — attack up close for max damage
🔥Dodge fire traps and spike floors
🏃Run as far as you can to earn distance
❤️You have 180 HP — don't waste it
✨ Artifacts — Found in Chests
[1] Iron Helm — +1 Max HP per pickup, applies instantly
[2] Shadow Berries — Instantly restores 5 HP on pickup
[3] Mana Crystal — Permanently increases sword damage by +1
[4] Fire Feather — Press 4 to activate fire shield for 10 seconds
[5] Ancient Core — A mysterious relic... reserved for the Boss encounter
Artifacts glow and rise from opened chests. Passive ones (1–3) apply instantly on pickup. Active ones (4–5) go to your hotbar — press the matching number key to use.
